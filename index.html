About me

My name is Nikola Valkov. I was born and raised in the beautiful city of Karlovo. I lived and spent my highschool years there where I studied "Systems Programming" at a school in a small town near Karlovo called Sopot. I graduated in 2023 with an Arduino embedded system project.

After I graduated school (this year) I decided that I was going to attend the university of Plovdiv "Paisii Hilendarski" where I am now studying "Software Engineering". Besides my academic work I also work on my own projects and constatly strive to learn and improve my abilities and my knowledge as a developer.

I am very passionate when it comes to developing high quallity software products and I am very keen on learning how to do that using the best industry pracitces and learning from other developers while helping with my own knowledge. I am always eager to contribute and help someone elses work or work in a team with other developers in the creation and maintanance of all kinds of projects. If you are a developer or an employer searching for an ambitious engineer, you can always contact me.
